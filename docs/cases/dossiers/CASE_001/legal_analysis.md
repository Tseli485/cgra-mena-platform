# Legal Analysis - Unaccompanied Minor - Syrian Refugee

## Legal Framework Applied


| Law/Regulation | How It Applies | Responsible Party | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| UN Convention on the Rights of the Child (CRC, 1989) | Amira is entitled to protection, care, and legal representation as a minor | Government Social Services + Nonprofits | Implemented |
| Convention Relating to the Status of Refugees (1951) | Amira qualifies for refugee status based on persecution in Syria | Immigration Authority + Legal Counsel | Pending/Granted |
| EU Directive 2011/95/EU | Defines refugee status and subsidiary protection in European context | National Immigration Services | Applied |
| Child Protection Laws (French/Local) | Mandates appointment of tuteur; establishes guardianship responsibilities | Court System + Social Services | Implemented |
| Trafficking in Persons Protocol (UN) | Protects minors from exploitation; provides safe harbor provisions | Law Enforcement + Social Services | Applied |
| Education Access Laws | Guarantees free primary and secondary education regardless of immigration status | Ministry of Education | Implemented |
| Medical Access Rights | Entitles all persons to emergency and essential medical care | Health Ministry + Hospitals | Implemented |
