# Documents Checklist - Additional Complex Case - Legal, Employment & Family Intersections

## Required Documents & Status

Based on the Solutions Implemented section:


1. **Emergency Medical Care and Treatment**
   - Immediate medical assessment and wound evaluation
   - Aggressive infection treatment: antibiotics, wound cleaning, monitoring
   - Surgical consultation if needed; assessment of permanent damage risk
   - Pain management during acute phase
   - Occupational therapy referral for hand rehabilitation and function recovery
   - Regular follow-up monitoring; weekly assessments during acute phase
   - Nutritional support to enhance healing (protein, micronutrients)
   - Documentation of all injuries and medical findings for legal proceedings

2. **Psychological Support and Trauma Recovery**
   - Referral to trauma-informed child psychologist
   - Initial psychosocial first aid; establishment of safety
   - Trauma-focused cognitive behavioral therapy (TF-CBT)
   - Individual counseling sessions 2x weekly initially; then weekly
   - Processing of abuse trauma, injury trauma, and identity disruption
   - Depression screening and treatment (medication referral if needed)
   - Coping skills development; stress management techniques
   - Peer support through group sessions with other trauma-affected youth

3. **Safety Assessment and Sheltering Decision**
   - Comprehensive safety assessment of home environment
   - Risk assessment: stepfather's presence and threat level
   - Initial protective decision: sheltering in child protection facility (temporary)
   - Safe space allowing psychological stabilization
   - Reassessment after household changes (stepfather removal)
   - Gradual transition back to home as safety improves
   - Ongoing safety monitoring; clear escalation procedures if risks emerge

4. **Educational Re-engagement and Catch-up Program**
   - Comprehensive educational assessment; baseline established
   - Recognition of severe academic delays (3-4 grades behind)
   - Non-formal education program: focus on basic literacy and numeracy
   - Intensive instruction in foundational skills; small group or individual lessons
   - Recognition that standard grade-level education not appropriate
   - Vocational education track identified as more suitable than grade-level return
   - Enrollment in second-chance education program (vocational track)
   - Peer reintegration support; social preparation for school re-entry
   - Coordination with school regarding Bashir's history and needs

5. **Family Assessment and Capacity Building**
   - Comprehensive family assessment; relationships, dynamics, strengths, challenges
   - Individual counseling for mother Amal addressing her own trauma and overwhelm
   - Psychoeducation for mother on trauma, abuse, child development
   - Parenting support focusing on trauma-informed approaches
   - Exploration of mother's economic opportunities and barriers
   - Assessment of father Ibrahim's capacity for involvement (if any)
   - Assessment of extended family's supportive roles
   - Clear family safety plan; roles and responsibilities defined

6. **Economic Restructuring and Alternative Livelihood**
   - Comprehensive economic assessment; income sources and needs documented
   - Removal of child labor as family income strategy (though economically painful)
   - Government emergency assistance application; cash support approved
   - Mother's employment search support; job training or assistance accessed
   - Exploration of father Ibrahim's financial obligations and contribution
   - Microfinance or small business assessment for mother's self-employment
   - Economic literacy and budgeting support for family
   - Gradual transition to sustainable income; monitoring for reversion to child labor

7. **Legal Investigation and Prosecution Support**
   - Police investigation of child labor violation
   - Investigation of abuse and assault charges against stepfather Karim
   - Evidence gathering: medical documentation, witness statements, labor records
   - Prosecution of employer Mustafa for child labor violations
   - Prosecution of stepfather Karim for assault and abuse
   - Victim support services for Bashir throughout legal process
   - Preparation of Bashir for potential testimony (sensitive to trauma)
   - Legal representation or advocacy support
   - Documentation that case is proceeding (though slowly)

8. **Occupational Rehabilitation and Long-term Health Monitoring**
   - Occupational therapy for hand function restoration
   - Physical exercises and rehabilitation for injured hand
   - Graded return to normal activities as healing progresses
   - Long-term monitoring of hand function; neurosensory assessment
   - Pain management and scar treatment if needed
   - Limitation documentation if permanent restrictions exist
   - Counseling on work modifications or future occupational choices (considering hand limitations)
   - Regular medical follow-up; connection to ongoing health care
