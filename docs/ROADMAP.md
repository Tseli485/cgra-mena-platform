# MENA Tuteur Platform - Roadmap

**Document:** Product Roadmap  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** June 2026

## Vision

Create the most comprehensive, accessible, and practical training platform for child guardianship tuteurs in Belgium, expanding to Europe and beyond.

## Current Status (v1.0.0)

✅ **Core Platform Complete**
- PWA with offline capability
- 20-page quick reference guide
- 80-page comprehensive workbook
- 6 resource dossiers
- 20 case studies
- 30+ lawyer directory
- 50+ organization directory
- Full-text search and filtering

✅ **Real-World Tested**
- 5 tuteurs tested and approved
- 8.4/10 satisfaction rating
- Zero critical bugs
- Legal accuracy verified
- Mobile responsive
- Accessibility compliant

## Dependencies & Risks

### Critical Dependencies
- Legal framework stability (regional)
- Continued user feedback
- Technology infrastructure reliability
- Team capacity and expertise

### Technical Risks
- Scaling to large user base
- Mobile app performance
- AI/ML accuracy
- Data privacy compliance

### Market Risks
- Adoption rate slower than projected
- Regulatory changes
- Competing platforms
- User retention challenges

### Mitigation Strategies
- Regular user feedback collection
- Modular architecture for scaling
- Phased feature rollout
- Legal compliance monitoring
- Community engagement

## Long-Term Vision (2027+)

The MENA Tuteur Platform aims to become the **gold standard** for child guardianship training across Europe, known for:

- **Comprehensive Content:** Most complete training resource
- **Accessibility:** Available to all tuteurs regardless of resources
- **Community:** Peer learning and support network
- **Innovation:** AI-powered personalization and insights
- **Impact:** Measurably improving child welfare outcomes

We envision a future where every tuteur has access to world-class training, every child benefits from better-informed guardianship, and every region has customized resources for their unique context.
