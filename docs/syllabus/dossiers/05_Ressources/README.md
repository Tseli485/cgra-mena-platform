# 05_Ressources

## Overview
This folder contains comprehensive contact directories and resource lists for organizations, legal advisors, and support services available to MENA and their guardians. Includes government agencies, NGOs, emergency services, and specialized support providers.

## Contents

### Avocats.md
Directory of lawyers and legal aid services specializing in refugee and asylum law. Includes:
- Legal aid (aide juridictionnelle) organizations
- Lawyers specializing in asylum and MENA protection
- Cost and fee structures
- How to request legal representation
- Language availability
- Regional variation of services

### Organisations.md
List of NGOs and civil society organizations supporting MENA. Covers:
- Child protection organizations (child rights advocacy)
- Refugee support organizations
- Integration and education support
- Mental health and trauma services
- Disability support services
- LGBTQ+ protection organizations

### Centres.md
Physical centers and facilities providing services. Includes:
- CADA (Centres d'Accueil pour Demandeurs d'Asile - asylum seeker housing)
- Reception centers for initial assessment
- Healthcare centers with migrant-focused services
- Education centers and language programs
- Vocational training centers
- Locations and access information

### Contacts_Urgence.md
Emergency contact information and hotlines. Covers:
- Police emergency (17)
- Medical emergency (15)
- Child protection emergency numbers
- Crisis counseling and mental health hotlines
- Missing person reporting procedures
- 24/7 available services

### Horaires_Services.md
Operating hours and accessibility information for key services. Includes:
- Government office hours (CGRA, Prefecture, Social Services)
- School calendar and hours
- Healthcare facility hours
- Legal aid office hours
- Public transportation schedules
- Holiday closures and emergency alternatives

## When to Use

- **Avocats.md**: When legal representation is needed or requested
- **Organisations.md**: When seeking specialized support services
- **Centres.md**: For housing, healthcare, or education referrals
- **Contacts_Urgence.md**: During emergencies or crises
- **Horaires_Services.md**: When scheduling appointments or services

## How to Print/Distribute

- Print **Contacts_Urgence.md** as a laminated card for all minors and staff
- Distribute **Avocats.md** to minors upon arrival (in their language)
- Keep **Centres.md** updated and accessible to all intake staff
- Post **Horaires_Services.md** in common areas for quick reference
- Provide **Organisations.md** links in digital formats when possible
- Review and update all resources quarterly for accuracy
