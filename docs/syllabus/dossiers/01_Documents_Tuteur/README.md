# 01_Documents_Tuteur

## Overview
This folder contains essential legal and administrative documents that tutors and guardians need to understand and maintain as part of their guardianship responsibilities for MENA (Minors Étrangers Non Accompagnés / Unaccompanied Foreign Minors).

## Contents

### Checklist_Tuteur.md
A comprehensive checklist of all obligations and responsibilities for tutors caring for MENA. Includes daily, weekly, and monthly tasks, documentation requirements, and compliance checkpoints.

### Obligations_Legales.md
Complete overview of legal obligations under French law (Code de l'action sociale et des familles). Covers:
- Duty of care and protection standards
- Reporting requirements (CRIP, authorities)
- Record-keeping obligations
- Educational and health monitoring
- Rights and responsibilities of guardianship

### Obligations_Financieres.md
Financial and budgetary guidance for tutors, including:
- Allowances and subsidies available
- Expense documentation requirements
- Bank account management
- Financial reporting to authorities
- Audit compliance

### Droits_MENA.md
Comprehensive guide to the rights of MENA under French law, including:
- Right to education and healthcare
- Right to legal representation
- Protection from exploitation
- Housing and accommodation rights
- Asylum procedure rights

## When to Use

- **Checklist_Tuteur.md**: Daily reference guide; print and post in your workspace
- **Obligations_Legales.md**: Before taking on guardianship; reference when unsure of legal requirements
- **Obligations_Financieres.md**: During budget planning and financial reporting periods
- **Droits_MENA.md**: When advocating for the minor's rights; share with the minor in age-appropriate language

## How to Print/Distribute

- Print **Checklist_Tuteur.md** as laminated A4 sheets for quick reference
- Distribute **Droits_MENA.md** to minors in their language when possible
- Keep **Obligations_Legales.md** and **Obligations_Financieres.md** in your records for audits
- Share with legal counsel and social workers as needed
