# MENA Tuteur Training Platform - Syllabus Package

**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Date:** June 2026  
**Status:** Production Ready

## Overview

This package contains the complete MENA Tuteur training syllabus and supporting resources. It includes:

- **Two PDF Formats:** Quick reference (20 pages) + Complete workbook (80 pages)
- **6 Dossier Folders:** Documents, forms, guides, case studies, resources, templates
- **Guides:** Usage guide and printing instructions
- **Digital & Print Ready:** All content optimized for both web and print distribution

## Quick Start

### For Digital Use
1. Download `SYLLABUS_COMPLETE.pdf` for comprehensive training
2. Use `SYLLABUS_COURTE.pdf` for quick reference and field use
3. Navigate dossiers/ for specific resources and templates

### For Print Distribution
1. Print `SYLLABUS_COURTE.pdf` (20 pages) as pocket reference
2. Print `SYLLABUS_COMPLETE.pdf` (80 pages) for detailed training
3. See `PRINTING_GUIDE.md` for optimal settings and binding options

## Dossier Folders

### 01_Documents_Tuteur
Foundational documents describing the tuteur role:
- Role descriptions and responsibilities
- Legal and ethical requirements
- Qualification and competency checklists
- Training paths and certification

### 02_Formulaires_CGRA
Official forms and administrative templates:
- Intake forms
- Reporting templates
- Case management forms
- Documentation requirements

### 03_Guides_Procedure
Step-by-step procedure guides:
- How to start tutorship
- Incident reporting procedures
- Escalation protocols

### 04_Cas_Pratiques
15+ detailed case studies covering:
- Age 0-3 scenarios (prenatal support, newborn care)

### 06_Templates_Lettres
Professional letter templates:
- Court correspondence

- Official reports
- Family contact templates

## How to Use This Package

### For Individual Tuteurs
1. Print or download `SYLLABUS_COURTE.pdf`
2. Keep it accessible for field reference
3. Refer to dossier folders for specific templates or procedures

### For Training Programs
1. Use `SYLLABUS_COMPLETE.pdf` as training curriculum
2. Supplement with case studies from 04_Cas_Pratiques/
3. Provide trainees with templates from 06_Templates_Lettres/

### For Organizations
1. Distribute `SYLLABUS_COURTE.pdf` to all staff

4. Reference lawyers/organizations from 05_Ressources/

## Printing Recommendations

**For Quick Reference (20-page):**
- Format: Pocket size or A5 (folded A4)
- Paper: 80g/m² white or cream
- Binding: Saddle-stitch or spiral
- Cost: ~€2-4 per copy

**For Comprehensive Workbook (80-page):**
- Format: A4 (standard letter)
- Paper: 80g/m² white
- Binding: Spiral or perfect-bound
- Cost: ~€8-12 per copy

See `PRINTING_GUIDE.md` for detailed printing specifications.
