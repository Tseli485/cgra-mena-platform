# 06_Templates_Lettres

## Overview
This folder contains templates for official correspondence from tutors, guardians, and minors to legal authorities, educational institutions, family members, and other parties. Each template provides professional formatting, legal language, and customizable sections.

## Contents

### Lettre_Avocat.md
Template for communication with legal counsel. Includes:
- Requesting legal representation
- Submitting case information to lawyer
- Following up on legal matters
- Payment and fee discussions
- Professional formatting requirements
- Confidentiality and privilege notes

### Lettre_Autorite.md
Template for official correspondence to government agencies. Covers:
- Letters to CGRA (Refugee Commission)
- Letters to Prefecture (administrative authority)
- Letters to Social Services (ASE - Aide Sociale à l'Enfance)
- Letters to CRIP (Child Protection Council)
- Formal complaint letters
- Proper formatting and tone

### Lettre_Ecole.md
Template for school and education correspondence. Includes:
- Enrollment requests and documentation
- Communication about educational progress
- Requesting accommodations or support
- Explaining absences or transfers
- Parent/tutor authorization letters
- Professional communication standards

### Lettre_Famille.md
Template for correspondence with family members. Covers:
- Initial contact introduction
- Status updates and information sharing
- Family reunification discussion
- Respectful family relationship guidance
- Safety and welfare considerations
- Handling sensitive situations

### Lettre_Reunion.md
Template for internal meeting communications. Includes:
- Meeting notes and documentation
- Follow-up action items
- Communication to all parties
- Formal record-keeping
- Decision notification letters
- Tutor coordination templates

## When to Use

- **Lettre_Avocat.md**: When initiating or maintaining legal representation
- **Lettre_Autorite.md**: For official correspondence with government bodies
- **Lettre_Ecole.md**: For school enrollment and educational matters
- **Lettre_Famille.md**: For family contact and communication
- **Lettre_Reunion.md**: For meeting documentation and follow-up

## How to Print/Distribute

- Print templates as single sheets or staple as a handbook
- Distribute digitally for easy customization
- Provide to minors (with tutor assistance) for their own correspondence
- Use as training examples for proper professional communication
- Keep completed letters in the minor's file for documentation
- Maintain digital copies for organizational records
