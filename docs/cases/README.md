# CASES Dossier: A Comprehensive Framework for Guardianship & Tutorship Cases

Welcome to the CASES Dossier—a meticulously researched and structured collection of 15 real-world guardianship and tutorship legal cases, complete with analysis, lessons, and implementation frameworks.

## What Is This?

The CASES Dossier provides a learning resource for legal professionals, guardians, social workers, and students studying guardianship law. Each case is presented as a complete dossier containing:

- **Main Case Narrative**: Factual summary and context
- **Timeline**: Key dates and events
- **Legal Analysis**: Framework, jurisdiction, and precedents
- **Problems Identified**: Issues encountered and their implications
- **Solutions Implemented**: Actual remedies and outcomes
- **Tuteur Obligations**: Guardian/tutorship responsibilities and duties
- **Key Lessons**: Takeaways and best practices
- **Resources**: Relevant legislation, forms, and references
- **Implementation Checklist**: Step-by-step guidance for practitioners

## How to Use This Dossier

### For Legal Research
1. Identify the legal issue or problem type
2. Use `CASE_FINDER.md` or `INDEX_BY_TYPE.md` to locate relevant cases
3. Read the full case dossier (main_case.md + legal_analysis.md)
4. Review `implementation_summary.md` for practical lessons
5. Check `resources.md` for legislation and precedents

### For Teaching & Training
1. Assign individual cases or case clusters by topic
2. Use `CASES_SUMMARY_TABLE.md` for overview lectures
3. Reference `CASE_FRAMEWORK.md` for analytical structure
4. Provide `documents_checklist.md` as training materials
5. Direct students to `tuteur_obligations.md` for duty guidance

### For Practice & Implementation
1. Identify your jurisdiction and case type
2. Review `implementation_summary.md` for procedural steps
3. Use `documents_checklist.md` to ensure compliance
4. Cross-reference `resources.md` for applicable legislation
5. Follow `tuteur_obligations.md` for ongoing responsibilities

### For Case Development
1. Review `GUIDE_COLLECTING_REAL_CASES.md` for methodology
2. Use `CASE_SCHEMA.json` for consistent data structure
3. Follow `CASE_FRAMEWORK.md` for analytical consistency
4. Maintain records using the 7-document format per case
