# 02_Formulaires_CGRA

## Overview
This folder contains official CGRA (Commission de Généraliste Refugiés et Apatrides / Refugee and Stateless Persons Committee) forms and submission documents required for asylum procedures, situation reporting, complaints, and age verification extensions.

## Contents

### Demande_Asile.md
Official asylum application form and submission procedures. Includes:
- Form structure and required fields
- Documentation checklist
- Submission deadlines
- Contact information for CGRA offices
- Appeals process if rejected

### Rapport_Situation.md
Periodic situation report form for ongoing asylum cases. Covers:
- Status updates on applicant circumstances
- Changes in family situation
- Employment/education updates
- Housing and financial status
- Health and welfare information

### Reclamation_Refus.md
Form and procedures for filing complaints against asylum refusal decisions. Includes:
- Grounds for appeal (substantive and procedural)
- Filing deadlines (30 days from refusal notice)
- Required documentation
- Administrative court procedures
- Legal representation requirements

### Extension_18ans.md
Application form for extending guardianship protection beyond age 18 (up to 21). Covers:
- Eligibility criteria
- Documentation requirements (medical, social, educational)
- Timeline for application (before age 18)
- Extensions available and durations
- Renewal procedures

## When to Use

- **Demande_Asile.md**: When a MENA first arrives and initiates asylum request
- **Rapport_Situation.md**: Quarterly or as required by CGRA; at each status check
- **Reclamation_Refus.md**: Immediately upon receiving asylum refusal decision
- **Extension_18ans.md**: When minor is 17-18 years old and nearing adulthood

## How to Print/Distribute

- Print **Demande_Asile.md** in French and other relevant languages
- Provide **Rapport_Situation.md** to the minor (or tutor on their behalf) before each due date
- Keep **Reclamation_Refus.md** readily accessible in case of refusal
- Distribute **Extension_18ans.md** at least 6 months before the minor's 18th birthday
- File originals with CGRA; keep copies for your records
