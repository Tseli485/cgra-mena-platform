# 03_Guides_Procedure

## Overview
This folder contains comprehensive step-by-step guides for major legal and administrative procedures affecting MENA, from asylum applications to education enrollment and housing arrangements. Each guide walks through the complete process with decision trees, timelines, and contact points.

## Contents

### Procedure_Asile_Complete.md
End-to-end guide to the French asylum procedure from arrival through final decision. Includes:
- Initial registration and orientation procedures
- CGRA application submission steps
- Interview preparation and conduct
- Decision timelines and notification processes
- Appeal procedures and options
- Post-approval integration steps

### Procedure_Famille.md
Procedures for family reunification and family-related matters. Covers:
- Family tracing and verification
- Family reunification eligibility and process
- Contact authorization procedures
- Travel authorization for family visits
- Legal guardianship modifications if family arrives

### Procedure_Logement.md
Housing arrangement and access procedures. Includes:
- Access to government housing (CADA, emergency housing)
- Applications and waiting list procedures
- Housing rights and responsibilities
- Private rental arrangements
- Housing for extended minors (18-21)

### Procedure_Ecole.md
Education enrollment and access procedures. Covers:
- School enrollment (primary, secondary, higher education)
- Language support programs (FLE - Français Langue Étrangère)
- Exam accommodations and qualifications recognition
- Extracurricular activities and sports access
- Continuation of education beyond age 18

## When to Use

- **Procedure_Asile_Complete.md**: Essential reading upon arrival; reference at each procedural step
- **Procedure_Famille.md**: When the minor expresses desire for family contact
- **Procedure_Logement.md**: At intake and before each housing transition
- **Procedure_Ecole.md**: Immediately upon arrival; updated each academic year

## How to Print/Distribute

- Print **Procedure_Asile_Complete.md** as a full handbook in the minor's language
- Distribute **Procedure_Ecole.md** to schools and educational advisors
- Share **Procedure_Logement.md** with housing coordinators and social workers
- Keep **Procedure_Famille.md** available for sensitive discussions
- Use flow charts from all procedures in orientation sessions
