## TABLE 5: Responsibility Distribution Matrix

### Tuteur's Role Across Phases

| Responsibility Area | Tuteur Role | Coordination Partner | Frequency |
|-------------------|------------|----------------------|-----------|
| **Identity & Legal Documentation** | Primary responsibility | Civil registry, Immigration, Court | One-time or as needed |
| **Health & Medical Care** | Arrange and monitor | Pediatrician, Specialists, Health system | Quarterly to monthly |
| **Education & School** | Liaison and advocate | School, Teachers, Coordinators | Monthly minimum |
| **Mental Health & Psychology** | Arrange and coordinate | Psychologist, Therapist, School | Monthly to quarterly |
| **Family Contact** | Facilitate and monitor | Family contact center, Social workers | Monthly to quarterly |
| **Financial Management** | Direct responsibility | Bank, Government benefits office | Monthly |
| **Case File Maintenance** | Primary responsibility | Social worker, MDT | Continuous |
| **Compliance & Reporting** | Primary responsibility | Child protection authority | Monthly to quarterly |
| **Care Facility Oversight** | Monitor and evaluate | Facility management, Social workers | Bi-monthly to monthly |
| **Multi-Disciplinary Coordination** | Organize meetings | School, Medical, Mental health, Social work | Semi-annual to annual |

### Government/Authority Role

| Area | Government Responsibility | Frequency |
|------|---------------------------|-----------|
| **Subsidies & Benefits** | Provide financial support (€1,200-2,200/month) | Monthly |
| **Legal Oversight** | Monitor guardianship compliance | Annual to bi-annual |
| **Child Protection** | Case file review and authority | Semi-annual |
| **Health System Access** | Provide/coordinate healthcare services | As needed |
| **Education System** | Provide/coordinate schooling | Continuous |
| **Family Tracing** | Assist with family location | As applicable |
| **Supervision & Inspection** | Facility and care quality monitoring | Annual |

### Child's Role (Age-Appropriate)

| Age Group | Child's Responsibilities |
|-----------|--------------------------|
| **0-2 years** | Participate in care routines, medical procedures, early attachment formation |
| **2-6 years** | School participation, following routines, cooperating with assessments, family contact |
| **6-12 years** | School attendance, academic engagement, therapy participation, family visits |
| **12-18 years** | School engagement, therapy participation, family decisions, independent skill building, life planning |

## TABLE 6: Quick Reference Grid for Practitioners

### Age-Based Checklist Grid

```
Age Group    | Primary Focus           | Main Obligation Category        | Key Resources Needed
0-2 years    | Stabilization           | Medical + Legal documentation   | Pediatrician, facility, family tracing
2-6 years    | Education + Language    | School enrollment + language    | Kindergarten, speech therapy
6-12 years   | School + Mental health  | Academics + psychological eval  | School, therapist, academic support
12-18 years  | Independence prep       | Reunification + vocational      | Secondary school, life skills program
```

### Service Access Priority

**URGENT (First 30 days):**
- Medical screening and stabilization
- Identity documentation
- Safe shelter placement
- Family contact initiation

**HIGH (First 3 months):**
- Comprehensive medical assessment
- Psychological evaluation
- Legal status determination
- Care plan development

**ONGOING:**
- Health monitoring (quarterly to annual)
- Educational progress (monthly)
- Psychological support (as needed)
- Family contact (monthly minimum)
- Progress reporting (quarterly)
