**CGRA Emergency Support**
- **Number**: +32 2 793 9911

### Decision Tree: When to Contact Whom

```
EMERGENCY?
├─ YES → Call 112 (life-threatening) or CGRA emergency +32 2 793 9911 (asylum urgent)
└─ NO → Continue below

ASYLUM-SPECIFIC?
├─ YES → CGRA office +32 2 793 8793 (status) or legal aid +32 2 500 5000
│        If time-sensitive (deadline) → +32 2 793 9911 (emergency line)
│        If want to appeal → Appeal Specialists +32 2 502 9999
└─ NO → Continue below

MEDICAL/HEALTH?
├─ YES → Emergency 112 (urgent) or clinic +32 2 405 0405 (non-urgent)
│        If mental health crisis → +32 2 648 4014
└─ NO → Continue below

HOUSING/SHELTER?
├─ YES → Caritas +32 2 515 8888 (long-term) or +32 800 90 100 (emergency shelter)
└─ NO → Continue below

LEGAL (non-asylum)?
├─ YES → Legal Aid Office +32 2 500 5000 (free) or local lawyer
└─ NO → Continue below

CHILD/FAMILY?
├─ YES → Children's Rights +32 2 673 2891 (protection/legal)
│        Family counseling → +32 2 534 3333
└─ NO → Continue below

EMPLOYMENT/TRAINING?
├─ YES → Employment Support +32 2 556 3333 or www.emploi-asile.be
└─ NO → Check regional office for your area

STILL UNSURE?
└─ Call Belgian Refugee Council +32 2 230 6350 (general referral) or
   Visit www.vluchteling.be (navigate to services) or
   Text "AIDE" to 50123 for SMS guidance
```

### Print Instructions

**How to use this card:**
1. **Print both sides** on card stock (if available) or regular paper
2. **Laminate** (optional, for durability)
3. **Cut along dotted lines** (if provided)
4. **Keep in wallet** with your other documents
5. **Share number with trusted contacts** so they can help you reach someone in emergency

**Languages available:**
- French version: www.vluchteling.be/cartes (French)
- Dutch version: www.vluchteling.be/kaarten (Dutch)
- English version: www.vluchteling.be/cards (English)
- Arabic version: www.vluchteling.be/البطاقات (Arabic)

**Update your card:**
- Every 6 months (lawyer changes, new contacts)
- When you move (update address)
- When legal status changes
- When you get new support worker
